# Projects

Ookra

I am co-founder and CTO of Ookra. I made everything including the backend, frontend, apps, and interface myself. Ookra lets photographers keep track of customer information through an app, and lets photographers upload all of their images at once to the Ookra site. The site then combines the information from the app with the timestamps of the uploaded pictures to contact all the customers with a page to buy their photographs.
