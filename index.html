# About Me My name is Alexandre Laplante, I am a software developer living in Ottawa. I have a Master's degree in Computer Science with specialization in Quantum Information from the university of Waterloo. I also have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a Minor in Physics from the University of Ottawa. My Master's Thesis presented an efficient parallel algorithm for solving a certain class of Semidefinite Programs, thus showing the containment of a quantum complexity class called XOR-MIP* in PSPACE.
